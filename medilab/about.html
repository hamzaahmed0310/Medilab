About

Medilab Medical Instruments Ltd. (OMI) is the UK based multinational corporation specializing in all-natural and drug-free medical devices, wellness and fitness equipment. OMI designs, manufactures and distributes each product to wholesalers and directly to consumers. With an expanding global reach, our company works with clients across Europe, the Middle East and now the U.S. and Canada to help customers achieve optimal health and wellness. All products of Medilab are developed and manufactured in Europe. Our American subsidiary handles distribution throughout the U.S and Canada.



OMI Products are engineered to promote healing and healthy living - without the use of drugs. Our products include medical devices and wellness, fitness equipment. Each OMI product has been specifically designed to accelerate self-healing through regeneration and recovery. We know that everybody has the ability to heal themselves, but each person's needs are unique. At OMI, we design a wide variety of products to help all people rediscover their balance with nature and experience optimal health.



Medilab never compromises quality. Our products are of the highest quality and clinically tested for safety. All OMI products meet the highest US FDA and EU CE standards. Research is fundamental to OMI. Our experts are constantly evaluating new results and market trends to ensure that our products are at the cutting-edge of medical technology.
